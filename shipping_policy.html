Shipping & Delivery Information

At Orange Foods, we strive to deliver your orders in the most efficient and timely manner. Please read our shipping policy carefully.

Shipping Partners

All orders for our users are shipped through:

  • Registered domestic courier companies
  • Speed Post (India Post)

We partner with trusted logistics providers to ensure safe and reliable delivery of your orders.

Shipping Timeframe

Orders are shipped within 1 day from the date of the order and/or payment.

Note: Actual delivery time may vary based on:

  • Courier company / post office norms
  • Destination location
  • Weather conditions
  • Other unforeseen circumstances

Delivery Address

Delivery of all orders will be made to the address provided by the buyer at the time of purchase.

Important:

  • Please ensure your delivery address is complete and accurate
  • Include landmarks if available
  • Provide correct contact number for delivery updates
  • Update address changes before order is shipped

Order Confirmation

Delivery of our services will be confirmed on your email ID as specified at the time of registration.

You will receive:

  • Order confirmation email
  • Shipping confirmation with tracking details
  • Delivery confirmation email

Shipping Charges

Shipping costs are calculated based on:

  • Product weight and dimensions
  • Delivery destination
  • Shipping method selected

Important: If there are any shipping cost(s) levied by the seller or the Platform Owner, the same is not refundable.

Delivery Limitations

Platform Owner shall not be liable for any delay in delivery by the courier company / postal authority.

Factors beyond our control include:

  • Natural disasters or extreme weather
  • Transportation delays
  • Customs clearance (if applicable)
  • Political unrest or strikes
  • Address errors or recipient unavailability

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS (if provided).

You can track your order by:

  • Clicking the tracking link in your email
  • Visiting the courier company's website with your tracking number
  • Contacting our customer support

Delivery Attempts

Our delivery partners typically make 2-3 delivery attempts:

  • First attempt: As per scheduled delivery date
  • Subsequent attempts: Within next 2-3 working days
  • If all attempts fail: Order returns to sender

Please ensure someone is available to receive the delivery during business hours.
